Asian Meeting of the Econometric Society

Leo Y. Yang
Image credit: Unsplash

Abstract

The 2019 Asia Meeting of the Econometric Society (2019 AMES) is open for registration. The meeting will be held on June 14-16, 2019 at Xiamen University, located in the resort-like island city of Xiamen (also called Amoy) in southeast China, with convenient flight connections to most major Asian cities. In addition to contributed and invited sessions, keynote lectures have been arranged.
